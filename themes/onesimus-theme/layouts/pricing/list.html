Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Community edition really free?

Yes. Onesimus is open source under the MIT license. The Community edition includes all core features and will always be free. You can build it from source or download pre-built packages.

What does Pro add over Community?

Pro adds features that matter for teams: a unified dashboard for multiple Directors, advanced reporting with charts, email notifications, and priority support. It also includes signed, tested binary packages so you don't have to build from source.

Can I try Pro before buying?

Yes — we offer a 30-day free trial of all Pro features. No credit card required. Contact us to get started.

Do you offer support contracts?

Yes. Both Pro and Enterprise include support. Enterprise customers get a dedicated support engineer and guaranteed response times via SLA.
